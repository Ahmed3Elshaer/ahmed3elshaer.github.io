About Me

I was passionate about technology since I was 12. During collage time it was my first time to be a Google Ambassador at my campus which enabled me to know more about Google and Android and this helped me to learn Android. I really hate our Educational system which led me to search for an Alternative, I choose to drop down a year from my Engineering study to learn Android perfectly. I loved the way that anyone at anyplace can learn whatever he wants for free and the way the MOOCs is made, so I decided to learn how to make MOOCs with Gamification and I did and became an Android Instructor. Currently, I'm 4+ years of experience working in Android development.
